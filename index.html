Our Services

Courts & Tribunals

Cases before Supreme Court, High Courts, Arbitration Tribunals, NCLT, MSME Councils, City Civil & District Courts, and more.

Arbitration & Mediation

Extensive practice in dispute resolution including invocation notice, claims, defence, S.9, S.11, S.17, S.34 and S.37 proceedings in domestic and international arbitration.

Insolvency & Companies Act

Claims under IBC, petitions under S.7, 9 and 10, representations for IRP, RP, Liquidators, and matters before NCLT and NCLAT.

Civil Recoveries

Recovery suits, summary suits, commercial suits, MSME claims before City Civil Courts, District Courts, and Small Causes Court.

Government Tenders & Contracts

Claims concerning government and PSU tenders with Indian Railways, IRCTC, NHAI, NHPC, RVNL, MES, and Municipal Corporations.

Labour & Employment

Workmen disputes, pension & gratuity, whistle-blower claims, confidentiality and non-compete clause disputes.

Shipping & Admiralty

Arrest of vessels, claims & lien against vessels, disputes with crew, shipping lines, freight forwarders before Admiralty Court.

Banking & NBFCs

Disputes arising from loan accounts, mortgages, title documents, foreclosure. Representations before RBI, DRT. Claims under NI Act, SARFAESI Act.

Criminal Litigation

Cases under NI Act, IPC, cyber-crimes, white collar crimes, private criminal complaints, and bail applications.

Intellectual Property Rights

Claims arising from trademarks, copyrights, patents and design law. Securing interim and final injunction orders.

Consumer & Insurance

Consumer claims before District, State and National forums. Marine, carrier, cargo, hull and machinery, P&I Club Insurance claims.

Advisory & Due Diligence

Legal advice and opinions over various transactions. Conducting Due Diligence of documents and compliances.

Incorporation

Drafting and vetting of incorporation documents, MOA, AOA, Partnership Deeds, LLP Deeds for Companies, LLPs and Partnerships.

Agreements & Contracts

Commercial contracts, Business Transfer, tender documents, EPC Contracts, lease deeds, JV Agreements, leave and license agreements.

Corporate Services

Employee contracts, code of conduct, workplace guidelines, offer letters, appointment letters, termination letters.

Transport Industry

Drafting and advising on transport issues, multi-modal transport, carrier acts, Bill of Lading documentation.

EPC Industry

Documentation, contracts, sub-contracts, employer disputes, and necessary correspondences for EPC projects.

Shipping Industry

Charter Party Agreements, Vessel Purchase Agreements, Offshore services contracts, customs and tariff, crew agreements.

Retainership Services

We provide comprehensive retainership services to various clients and corporates for non-litigation works on a monthly retainership basis. This ensures your business always has access to expert legal counsel when needed.
