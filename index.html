Services

What We Do

Litigation

⚖️

Courts & Tribunals

Cases before Supreme Court, High Courts, Arbitration Tribunals, NCLT, MSME Councils, City Civil & District Courts for corporates, MNCs, and PSUs.

🏛️

Arbitration & Mediation

Dispute resolution including invocation, claims, defence, S.9, S.11, S.17, S.34 and S.37 proceedings in domestic and international arbitration.

📋

Insolvency & IBC

Petitions under S.7, 9, 10 of IBC, representations for IRP, RP, Liquidators, corporate frauds before NCLT and NCLAT.

🏗️

Government Tenders

Claims with Indian Railways, IRCTC, NHAI, NHPC, RVNL, MES, MahaRail, MRIDC, MBPT, and Municipal Corporations.

🚢

Shipping & Admiralty

Arrest of vessels, claims & lien, disputes with crew, shipping lines, freight forwarders before Admiralty Court and DG Shipping.

🏦

Banking & Criminal

Loan disputes, mortgages, SARFAESI. Criminal cases under NI Act, IPC, cyber-crimes, white collar crimes, and bail applications.

💡

IP & Consumer

Trademark, copyright, patent disputes. Consumer claims including marine, cargo, hull insurance before District, State and National forums.

👥

Labour & Employment

Workmen disputes, pension, gratuity, whistle-blower, confidentiality, non-compete, negligence and misconduct claims.

Non-Litigation

🔍

Advisory & Due Diligence

Legal opinions over transactions. Due Diligence of documents and compliances. Advising on commercial negotiations.

📝

Agreements & Contracts

Commercial contracts, EPC, government contracts, lease deeds, JV Agreements, leave & license, vendor contracts.

🏢

Incorporation

MOA, AOA, Incorporation Deeds, Partnership Deeds, LLP Deeds for Companies, LLPs, and Partnerships.

⚙️

Industry Services

Shipping: Charter Party, Vessel Purchase, Offshore contracts. EPC: documentation, sub-contracts. Transport: multi-modal, carrier acts.

Retainership Services

Comprehensive retainership for corporates on a monthly basis — ensuring your business always has access to expert legal counsel when needed.
